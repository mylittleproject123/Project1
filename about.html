About Swappie

Your trusted partner for premium refurbished technology

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Swappie has become the leading provider of certified refurbished technology across Central America and the Caribbean. We believe that everyone deserves access to premium technology at affordable prices.

Our mission is to make high-quality refurbished devices accessible to everyone while promoting sustainability through the circular economy. Every device we sell undergoes rigorous testing and comes with a comprehensive warranty.

With locations across Nicaragua, Honduras, Trinidad & Tobago, and the USA, we're committed to bringing you the best technology with the best service.

Our Values

Quality Assurance

Every device undergoes comprehensive testing and certification before reaching our customers.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing electronic waste through responsible refurbishment and recycling.

Customer First

Your satisfaction is our priority with 1-year warranty and 30-day return guarantee.

Trust & Transparency

Honest pricing, detailed device conditions, and transparent business practices.
