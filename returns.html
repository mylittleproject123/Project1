30-Day Return Guarantee

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to return your device for a full refund. No questions asked if the device is in original condition.

Full Refund

Receive a complete refund of your purchase price, including original shipping costs. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Returnable Conditions

  • Device in original condition
  • All original accessories included
  • Original packaging and manuals
  • No additional software installed
  • Factory reset completed
  • No physical damage
  • Return within 30 days
  • Valid purchase receipt

Non-Returnable Conditions

  • Physical damage (drops, cracks)
  • Water or liquid damage
  • Missing accessories or packaging
  • Signs of excessive wear
  • Modified or jailbroken devices
  • Expired 30-day window
  • Cannot verify purchase
  • Devices sold "as-is" or final sale

Important Return Information

Free Return Shipping

We provide prepaid return shipping labels for all returns within our service areas.

Processing Time

Returns are processed within 2-3 business days of receipt. Refunds take 5-7 business days.

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for the same model in different colors or storage capacity, subject to availability.

Warranty vs Returns

Returns are for unwanted purchases. Defective devices are covered under our warranty policy.
